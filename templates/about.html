ABOUT US

Courses

We offer undergraduate courses such as business management, journalism, Information Technology courses, masters and PhD programmes. We also provide online programs for students who can't access the school physically

Location

Our main campus is located at Githurai town in Kiambu county. We also have 2 more branches, one at Kariobangi in Nairobi county and the other at Naivasha, Nakuru county

History

Our institution was founded in 1990 by famous business man Donald Davenport Kamau together with his brother Douglas Davenport Kamau
